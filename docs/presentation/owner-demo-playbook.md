# Owner Demo Playbook

This file is legacy presentation guidance from the earlier sample/demo phase.

It is not the current operational route map for The Dosa Spot or the production runtime.

## Official route

- Present first: `/`
- Preset: `Signature Atelier`
- Why: strongest balance between premium first impression, clear service framing, and a close that already feels practical.

## Secondary route

- Show second: `/demo/quay-poster/`
- Preset: `Poster Evening`
- Use when: the owner responds better to higher contrast, stronger atmosphere, and a more editorial mood.

## Fallback proof route

- Keep as proof: `/demo/northside-monolith/`
- Preset: `Northside Monolith`
- Use when: you need to show that the system still looks premium even before a business has full photography, contact data, or a richer brief.

## 30-90 second walkthrough

1. Open on the hero and let the first screen do the work.
2. Scroll to the owner-facing summary just below the hero.
3. Show the services section to prove the site is useful, not just attractive.
4. Scroll through atmosphere briefly to reinforce positioning and taste.
5. Close on the final CTA and explain that the remaining work is mostly real business data, photography, and local details.

## Closing line

Use a calm close:

`This is already the premium structure. The final step is simply replacing the sample layer with your real services, images, contact details, and local information.`

## Share guidance

- WhatsApp / link share: send the root route `/`
- In-person mobile walkthrough: keep the preset dock closed unless the owner asks for an alternate version
- Desktop follow-up: use `Poster Evening` second if you want to show the same brand in a moodier direction
