# Visual Families

## Food Warm Editorial

- Best for: restaurants, cafes, bakeries with strong food imagery and warm local energy.
- Personality: tactile, appetite-led, premium-casual.
- Typography: serif headline plus grounded sans body.
- Hero: food-first, editorial poster or split.
- Cards: editorial food cards and warm luxe panels.
- Gallery: mosaic or curated carousel.

## Local Service Premium

- Best for: local service, fitness, grooming, and practical premium businesses.
- Personality: clear, polished, useful, trustworthy.
- Typography: strong sans-led hierarchy.
- Hero: service-first or local-presence-first.
- Cards: clean elevated service cards and trust/contact panels.
- Gallery: limited grid or restrained carousel.

## Retail Clean Modern

- Best for: product-led shops and modern boutiques.
- Personality: clean, bright, controlled, modern.
- Typography: precise sans-led hierarchy.
- Hero: product-first.
- Cards: clean elevated product and info cards.
- Gallery: structured grid with product bias.

## Clinic Calm Trust

- Best for: clinic, health, and wellbeing businesses.
- Personality: calm, legible, authority-led, low-drama.
- Typography: calm sans hierarchy.
- Hero: trust-first.
- Cards: credential and reassurance panels.
- Gallery: limited proof strip focused on team, space, and exterior.

## Dark Boutique Luxury

- Best for: boutique grooming, premium retail, and visually rich wellness brands.
- Personality: cinematic, contrast-led, exclusive.
- Typography: display serif with cleaner sans support.
- Hero: editorial-first.
- Cards: editorial frames and darker luxe panels.
- Gallery: poster strip or image-led mosaic.

## Neutral Reusable

- Best for: mixed-signal businesses or incomplete briefs.
- Personality: restrained, flexible, low-risk.
- Typography: clean sans-led hierarchy.
- Hero: local-presence-first.
- Cards: clean elevated defaults.
- Gallery: small structured grid or restrained carousel.

## Why Only These Families

The goal is not infinite theming.

These six families cover the real visual directions the current system needs:

- warm food
- practical premium local services
- modern retail
- calm clinical trust
- darker boutique luxury
- neutral fallback
